Site Plan by Fernando Gonzalez - WDD131

Site Name: Puertas y Ventanas Helyver

The name was chosen by the client, who wants both the domain and the name of his site to specifically reflect what he does in addition to bearing his name.

puertasyventanas.cl

Site Purpose: For the Puertas y Ventanas Helyver

The Puertas y Ventanas Helyver website provides a hub for custom window and door installation services in the Santiago Metropolitan Region. The site offers detailed information on the various products and services available, including aluminum windows and doors, mirrors, and custom office structures.

Scenarios:

  • How can I get a quick estimate for installing new aluminum windows in my home?
  • Do you offer services for removing old windows and replacing them with new ones?
  • What types of mirrors do you offer, and can they be customized?
  • Do you provide services for office partitions and custom aluminum structures?
